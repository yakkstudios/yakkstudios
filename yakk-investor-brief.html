11 — RISK FACTORS
Honest Disclosure
Regulatory Risk — Crypto Classification
$YST is a community memecoin with utility mechanics. Token tax and revenue share features are subject to evolving regulatory frameworks. YAKK does not custody user funds; risk exposure is limited to platform fees and protocol-level operations.
Technical Risk — Single-File Architecture
Current implementation is a single-file HTML dapp — demonstrating speed of execution but creating scalability constraints. Migration to component-based architecture (React/Next.js) is on the roadmap for Q3 2026 to enable testing, CI/CD, and team scaling.
API Dependency Risk
Several features depend on public APIs (Solscan, Birdeye, DEX Screener, Magic Eden) that may apply rate limits at scale. Mitigation: dedicated RPC node procurement and premium API key acquisition are budgeted in the treasury roadmap.
Defamation / Legal Risk — Certified Clowns
The Certified Clowns list names specific accounts. All entries are clearly labelled as community opinion and on-s ain behavioural research, not legal findings. Legal review and strengthened disclaimers are in place. Protected as community commentary and financial research.
