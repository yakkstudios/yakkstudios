How it works

The Full Extraction Stack — A Field Guide

For every investigation the Cabal has published, the same architecture repeats. Understanding the mechanics protects you.

1. Hub-and-Spoke Bundling

One or two origin wallets distribute supply to hundreds of addresses before public trading begins. On-chain, each spoke address looks like an independent market participant. Together, they represent a coordinated supply block that was positioned before retail entered. In $PUMP: Hub 1 (77 wallets) + Hub 2 (851 wallets) = 928 pre-seeded addresses, $4.02B distributed.

2. Volume Farming

After the initial price discovery window, certain wallets begin cycling tokens in high-frequency, near-balanced patterns. The goal is to maintain volume metrics on aggregator platforms (DEXScreener, Birdeye) that retail traders use to assess activity. In $PUMP, March 2026 had 481,531 transactions at $1.17B — more transactions than July's launch month at a fraction of the USD volume. The farming machinery was still running 9 months post-launch.

3. Relay Routing ("The Chain")

Large transfers pass through one or more intermediate wallets in rapid succession — typically within 30–120 seconds. This creates the on-chain appearance of multiple independent actors while keeping the ultimate destination within the same coordinated cluster. Used for "buyback" narratives, coordinated selling into pumped price action, and obfuscating the source of large exits.

4. Silent Accumulation + Overhang

Some wallets receive large allocations and then go completely silent — no outbound transfers at all. These wallets represent potential future supply that can enter the market at any time. In $PUMP, 12 such wallets hold a combined $1.63B. Retail holding at current prices has this overhang above them regardless of any price action.

5. Narrative Deployment

Coordinated on-chain events — relay chains completing in seconds — are timed to coincide with publicly deployed narratives. When the chart goes green and Telegram channels announce "the team is buying back," tracing the actual transfer chain may reveal supply that was already in the ecosystem being moved between coordinated wallets. The narrative is the retail hook. The chain is the mechanism.
